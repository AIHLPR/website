CareerOS

The problem

Careers managed on guesswork.

Most professionals manage their careers reactively — they update a CV when they need a new job, apply to roles without understanding their real positioning, and have no idea whether they are aiming too low, too high, or at the wrong targets entirely.

The decisions that matter most — when to move, where to aim, what to learn next — get made on intuition. CVs sit static. Strengths go unsurfaced. Rejection patterns repeat invisibly.

CareerOS replaces guesswork with structured intelligence. A career is a graph, not a document.

The product

Five surfaces that turn a CV into intelligence.

Surface 01

Reconstruct the career, don't just store the CV.

Upload a PDF or DOCX. AI extracts and structures experience, skills, education, certifications — then reconstructs the narrative: trajectory, seniority band, industry positioning, the strengths that aren't obvious from the raw text.

Profile reconstruction
Surface 02

See the orbit, switch the lens.

The Orbit is a visual career map: skills, roles, and adjacencies clustered by domain. Four lenses overlay it — current profile, ideal archetypes, top-performer benchmarks, and ghost jobs (live open roles that match your trajectory).

Orbit map · 4 lenses
Surface 03

Score the fit before you apply.

Paste a job description. Get multi-dimensional fit analysis — skills, experience depth, education alignment, geography — an overall score, a clear apply / maybe / don't-apply call, and better-fitting alternatives if the match is weak.

Fit scoring · JD analysis
Surface 04

Ask an advisor that knows your profile.

A conversational AI grounded in your reconstructed career. "Position me for Head of Product." "Why have I been rejected from five similar roles?" Conversations persist; insight compounds.

Advisor chat · context-aware
Surface 05

Generate the documents from verified truth.

Role-specific CVs, optimised cover letters, LinkedIn summaries — generated from the structured profile, not invented. Every application leaves the platform faster than it would have left a blank page.

Document generation · pipeline tracking
What we built

One profile, four product surfaces.

Mobile-first, warm aesthetic on the outside — cream backgrounds, teal accents, rounded surfaces. The Orbit interface stays in a distinctive dark-mode language so the career map reads as its own product space.

Orbit interface

Dark-mode career map with colour-coded evidence nodes and source tags (CV · LinkedIn · GitHub · portfolio). Four lenses overlay live.

Fit analyser

Multi-dimensional scoring across skills, experience depth, education, and geography. Apply / maybe / don't, with reasoning.

Advisor chat

Context-aware conversational AI grounded in the reconstructed profile. Threads persist; insights compound.

Document studio

Role-specific CVs, cover letters, LinkedIn summaries — generated from verified profile data, not invented from prompts.

The stack

Managed infrastructure. Serverless AI. Zero ops overhead.

Frontend React 18 · Vite · TypeScript · Tailwind Responsive SPA. Mobile-first. Warm design system.
Backend Lovable Cloud · managed Supabase PostgreSQL with Row Level Security, real-time, auth.
AI layer 13+ serverless edge functions Parsing, extraction, scoring, chat, generation — all on one gateway.
Storage Secure file storage CV uploads and generated documents.
Security Role-based access · RLS Dedicated user_roles table, security-definer functions, no client-side privilege checks.
Applicable to

The same intelligence layer, other workforce surfaces.

A reconstructed career graph + a scoring engine + a context-aware advisor is not specific to job-seekers. The pattern applies to any workforce decision that's currently made on intuition.

Internal mobility & L&D Recruiter intelligence tools Executive coaching platforms Workforce planning Talent analytics dashboards Succession & promotion review
