Where it got stuck

Launches were ready. The listings weren’t.

The photography, copy, and launch dates were all in place. What got stuck was turning those inputs into Amazon-ready PDP and A+ modules. The internal digital content team was overloaded, so the work was briefed out to an external design agency — adding rounds of revisions, coordination, and waiting time. Every week a listing sat dark in the backlog was a week of lost sales.

Backlog Revision loops Time-to-market loss
How we approached it

Automate the repeatable. Keep judgement with the team.

We didn’t try to automate the creative call. We automated the structural assembly around it. Brand rules, padding, type sizes, layout patterns — the parts of the work that are repeatable — get applied by the workflow. The team’s reviewers stay in control of the decisions that actually need judgement: the look, the fit, the sign-off before anything goes out.
