The problem

Product launches were ready before the listing assets were.

For product teams, the launch bottleneck often appears after the product, photography, and copy are already approved. Amazon modules still need to be assembled, adapted, checked, localised, and exported across formats.

The work is repetitive, but the quality bar is high. Product images cannot be altered. Brand rules must stay intact. Claims need to be reviewed. Each market needs content that feels local without drifting from the approved brand system.

The team needed a guided production workflow that could reduce repeated design cycles without removing human control from the final decision.

The approach

Automation for the repeatable work. Review for the decisions that matter.

Step 01

Start from approved product inputs.

The workflow begins with approved product images, structured copy, and reference assets. The system does not invent or alter the product image; it uses the approved material as the source of truth.

Approved assets · Source control
Step 02

Generate Amazon-ready module layouts.

The system proposes desktop and mobile module compositions using brand rules, layout patterns, callout logic, and product-specific copy.

Layout engine · Retailer modules
Step 03

Keep brand and product checks inside the workflow.

Brand, product, and regional reviewers can check the output before export. The system supports faster production, but approval remains with the team.

Human review · Brand control
Step 04

Export assets for market-ready use.

Once approved, the workflow prepares structured outputs for Amazon PDP and A+ use, reducing the need to rebuild the same asset set manually for each product or market.

Export workflow · Market readiness
What we built

A guided asset-production workflow for Amazon launches.

Designed around the way product and marketing teams already work: approved inputs come in, module options are generated, humans review, and final assets move toward retailer-ready export.

Product input layer

A structured way to bring together approved product images, product copy, feature notes, and reference examples before asset generation begins.

Module composition layer

A layout workflow for creating Amazon-ready desktop and mobile modules using approved product images, callouts, annotations, and brand-safe composition logic.

Review layer

A human-in-the-loop review path so brand, product, and market teams can check outputs before they are used externally.

Export layer

A repeatable output process designed to reduce manual rebuilds and support consistent asset production across SKUs and markets.

Applicable to

The same pattern, in adjacent product teams.

The pattern, approved product inputs, guided composition, human review, and retailer-ready export, applies to any team producing high-volume product assets under brand and marketplace constraints.
