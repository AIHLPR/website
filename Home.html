FAQ

Questions we get asked early.

If yours is not here, the readiness check is the fastest way to answer it.

How do you charge? +
Most engagements start with a fixed price readiness check. From there, we agree on a scope and a delivery shape. We do not sell hours. We sell defined outcomes against milestones, with weekly check ins.
How long until something runs in production? +
Six to twelve weeks for a first working system, depending on what we are connecting to and how clean the data is. The readiness check tells us the realistic shape before either side commits.
Where does our data live? +
With you. Never with us. Everything we build runs inside your cloud tenancy (AWS, Azure, GCP) or on prem if you need that. EU hosted by default. Nothing leaves your environment, including during development.
Who owns the work? +
You do. The code, the models, the documentation, the prompts. We deliver it, write it up, and hand it over. There is no platform you have to keep paying us for.
What about the EU AI Act? +
Risk classification is part of the readiness check. We document the system from day one and run DPIAs with your DPO whenever personal data is involved. Compliance is how we build, not a workstream that gets added later.
Why work with you instead of a generalist consultancy? +
We build what we recommend. The roadmap and the production system come from the same team. You will not hand a strategy deck to someone else who has never deployed an LLM.
