Operational reality

From prototype to production.

AI prototypes often begin as internal tools, AI copilots, workflow automations, experimental systems, vibe-coded products, or operational use cases. Production environments require significantly more structure.

Engineering scars

Production is the boring engineering.

Rollbacks, fallbacks, drift, evaluation regressions, cost spikes, model switches. The systems people trust are the ones that have survived all of it.
